About Us

Coffee is one of the most popular drinks in the world, and here at Hackney Coffee we are dedicated to giving you premium java for a premium price.

Our cafe launched on the 10th October 2020, (after the covid pandemic restrictions had been revoked) as a chance for the community of Hackney to come together and contribute towards a place where we can relax and share some positivity. Our ingredients are sourced fresh and organic all around the world, so you can find confidence in knowing that all our menu items are healthy, tasty and give you the energy you need! And for those that aren't big coffee drinkers or are simply looking for a place to relax, we encourage you to come and say hi, and give our other menu items a try for yourself!

Why we're different

Feel healthy

All our ingredients are organically sourced, produced, cultivated and grown from different farms and stations across the world, to supply you with the best combo of vitamins, minerals and energy you need.

Enjoy

Because our ingredients are sourced from the most suitable places you can enjoy an experience of luxury at an affordable price!

Be social

Looking for a place to share your hobbies and find others who share the same hobbies? No worries! Our cafe is used by many in our community, all of different backgrounds, cultures and interests.

Relax

If you're just looking for a place to chill out, we recommend the mornings, when the cafe is less packed and you can enjoy a quiet spot to yourself!
